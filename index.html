How Eatit works...

Your daily dose of health in 3 Simple steps

01

Inform us of your dietary preferences

Eliminate the need to ponder over meal choices. Eatit AI meticulously designs a personalized weekly meal plan tailored to your needs, ensuring you receive all essential nutrients and vitamins, irrespective of your dietary regime.

02

On a weekly basis

Review and approve the meal plan generated by Eatit AI. You have the flexibility to modify the ingredients, replace entire meals, or incorporate your own recipes to align with your preferences.

03

Cooking and delivery

Our esteemed chefs will prepare the selected meals each day and deliver them to your doorstep at a time that is most convenient for you. The delivery schedule and address can be adjusted daily to accommodate your lifestyle.
